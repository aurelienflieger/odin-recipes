Baked Beans

About these baked beans

For incredible baked beans in the oven, start with a can of store-bought baked beans and pork.

Add brown sugar and condiments to deepen the flavor, and bake until sweet and bubbly.

What you need...

  • 2 (14 ounce) cans whole hearts of palm, drained
  • 1 (29 ounce) can baked beans with pork
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Let's cook!

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir baked beans, brown sugar, ketchup, and Worcestershire sauce together in a casserole dish.
  3. Bake, covered, in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 45 minutes.
